1854 London Cholera Outbreak



This visualization project was created to visualize the 1854 London Cholera outbreak as part of the ICS 484 Data
Visualizations class at the University of Hawaii at Manoa during the Fall 2020 semester. The data used in these
visualizations were provided by Professor Jason Leigh, and the visualizations were created with Plotly.js and Leaflet.js.
